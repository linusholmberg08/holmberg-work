No. II — A Note from the Editor

Why we make these guides.

The Field Guides are part of That Declutter Life, a small journal on decluttering and the patient keeping of a home. They are our longest pieces — the ones that demand a chair, a cup, and a spare half-hour — and they are how we work out what we actually think about a room.

Home organisation sits at the slow intersection of psychology, design, and the small daily habits that make a household possible. We read across all three, and we are skeptical of anyone selling certainty in any of them.

Researched, then written. Sources are named. Studies are cited. The limits are said out loud.
Useful by the end. You finish, you set the page down, and you know what to do next.
Honestly held. No affiliate clutter. No sponsored shelves. If we like it, we say so plainly.
