## Column notes

- `Title` — short working title, not the full caption
- `Publish` — date (and local time, if useful) the video went live on TikTok
- `TT 24h` / `TT 72h` — TikTok plays at 24 and 72 hours after publishing
- `YT 24h` / `YT 72h` — YouTube Shorts views at 24 and 72 hours after publishing
- `Likes` / `Comments` — totals across both platforms at the 72h check, unless split out in notes
- `Follows` — new followers in the 72h window, summed across platforms
- `Notes` — anything unusual: anomalies, comment trends, audio chosen, source of inbound traffic
