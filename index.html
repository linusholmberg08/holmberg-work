The idea

Most decluttering advice starts too late.

It tells you which box to buy, which drawer to empty, or which checklist to follow. Useful, sometimes. But it rarely asks why the drawer became a museum of abandoned intentions in the first place.

This project starts there: with identity, attention, desire, fear, and the honest question of what your home is trying to say back to you.
